Comparison of H.264 and VC-1

H.264 and VC-1 are popular video compression standards gaining use in the industry {{as of|...}}.

Overview

!VC-1

H.264

Goals

Designed to offer very high image quality with excellent compression efficiency<ref>...</ref>

Designed to meet a variety of industry needs with many profiles and levels, allowing for varying compression, quality and CPU usage levels, where the lowest level is for portable devices, designed with low CPU usage in mind, while the high levels are designed with very high quality and compression efficiency in mind{{citation needed|...}}
